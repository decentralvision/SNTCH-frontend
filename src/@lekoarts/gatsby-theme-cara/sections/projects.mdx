## Features

<ProjectCard
  title="Smart"
  link=""
  bg="linear-gradient(to right, darkred 0%, red 100%)"
>
  SNTCH collects data from all available sources and compiles it into a database of collective public knowledge. SNTCH links uniquely identifying data using a machine learning algorithm into a comprehensive profile.
  <br />
  <br />
  <FontAwesomeIcon icon={faBrain} size='6x' />
  <br />

</ProjectCard>

<ProjectCard
  title="Permanent"
  link=""
  bg="linear-gradient(to right, maroon 0%, orange 100%)"
>

  Using Ethereum on the back-end means that reports are stored permanently in a cutting edge, distributed, fault-tolerant, public database. This means that the records of reports made on SNTCH cannot be destroyed or edited. 
  
  <br />
  <FontAwesomeIcon icon={faMountain} size='10x' />

</ProjectCard>

<ProjectCard 
  title="Anonymous"
  link=""
  bg="linear-gradient(to right, darkorange 0%, darkred 100%)"
>

  All reports made to SNTCH are completely anonymous. Identity verification is only required to participate in managing and moderating the platform. Blockchain enables anonymous payments. 
  <br />
  <FontAwesomeIcon icon={faUserSecret} size='10x' />
  
  
</ProjectCard>

<ProjectCard
  title="Engaged"
  link=""
  bg="linear-gradient(to right, darkred 0%, maroon 100%)"
>
  Individual reports are available for a fiat (normal money) or crypto fee. Users are paid anonymously directly to their ethereum wallet for contributing their report.
  <br />
  <br />
  <FontAwesomeIcon icon={faUsers} size='7x' />

</ProjectCard>
